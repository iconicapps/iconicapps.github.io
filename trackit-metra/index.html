About TrackIt Metra

TrackIt Metra is the ultimate companion for Chicago Metra commuters. Get real-time train tracking, live arrival times, and comprehensive schedule information for all Metra lines. Whether you're a daily commuter or an occasional rider, our app helps you plan your journey with confidence.

Stay informed about delays, track changes, and service updates to ensure you never miss your train or arrive at the station too early.
