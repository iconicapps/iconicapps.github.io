Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Iconic Apps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TrackIt Metra mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers through the TrackIt Metra app.

Location Data: The app may request location permissions to provide you with relevant train information for nearby stations. Location data is used only for this purpose and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not identify individual users.

Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as device model, operating system version, and app version for technical support and app optimization.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide real-time train tracking and schedule information
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To provide technical support and customer service
  • To analyze app usage patterns and performance
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your favorite stations and routes are stored locally on your device. We do not store this information on our servers.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information.

5. Third-Party Services

The TrackIt Metra app may use third-party services such as:

  • Metra's public API for train data
  • Analytics providers for app performance monitoring
  • Location services for station proximity features

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Location Services

If you enable location services, the app may access your device's location to:

  • Find nearby Metra stations
  • Provide relevant train information for your location
  • Improve the accuracy of arrival time estimates

You can disable location services at any time through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The TrackIt Metra app is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
